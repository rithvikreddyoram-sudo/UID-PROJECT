Our Story

Crafted With Passion,
Trusted by Champions

SS Cricket was founded in 1969 in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh — the cricket equipment capital of the world. For over five decades, we have been handcrafting bats, pads, gloves, and protective gear for cricketers at all levels.

From gully cricket to Test matches, SS Cricket has been the trusted companion of players who dream big. Our artisans bring decades of experience to every piece they make.
